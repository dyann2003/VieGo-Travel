Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours, booking process, and travel in Vietnam.

How can we help you?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Contact our customer support team for assistance.

Email Us support@traveloka.com
Live Chat Available 24/7
How do I book a tour on your website?

You can book a tour by selecting your desired tour, choosing the date and number of participants, and completing the payment process on our website.

Can I book a tour for someone else?

Yes, you can book a tour for someone else by providing their details during the booking process.

How far in advance should I book my tour?

We recommend booking at least 3 days in advance to ensure availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal.

Is my booking confirmed immediately?

Yes, your booking is confirmed immediately after successful payment.

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking by logging into your account and selecting the booking you want to cancel.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Cancellation fees depend on the timing of your cancellation. Please check our policy for details.

How long does it take to get a refund?

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation approval.

Can I change my booking instead of canceling?

Yes, you can request changes to your booking by contacting our support team.

What happens if the tour is canceled by the operator?

You will receive a full refund or an option to reschedule.

What types of tours do you offer?

We offer city tours, adventure tours, cultural tours, and more.

Are the tours suitable for children?

Many of our tours are family-friendly. Please check the tour details for age restrictions.

Can I customize a tour?

Yes, contact us to create a custom tour tailored to your preferences.

What is included in the tour price?

Tour price typically includes transportation, guide, and entrance fees. Check each tour for specifics.

How do I prepare for a tour?

Wear comfortable clothes, bring water, and follow any instructions provided in your booking confirmation.

What is the best time to visit Vietnam?

The best time to visit is from November to April when the weather is dry and cool.

Do I need a visa to travel to Vietnam?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Please check with the Vietnamese embassy or consulate.

What currency is used in Vietnam?

The official currency is the Vietnamese Dong (VND).

Is it safe to travel in Vietnam?

Vietnam is generally safe for tourists, but always take standard precautions.

What languages are spoken in Vietnam?

Vietnamese is the official language. English is commonly spoken in tourist areas.
